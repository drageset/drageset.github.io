About Olve Drageset

I am an award winning and published artificial intelligence (AI) researcher, looking for work as a developer or research engineer in Oslo, Norway.

In 2019 I published at IEEE Conference on Games and the AAAI Artificial Intelligence for Interactive Digital Entertainment Conference. The latter work won the "Best Artifact" award at AIIDE 2019.

I am a curious self starter, who loves aquiring knowledge and sharing it with others. My experience as a research assistant programmer, AI intern and teaching assistant has given me experience working independently and in teams. In my free time I like to build things and have fun with friends: As a kid I was building longboards and ski bikes to hit the hills with my buddies, nowadays I'm building games and hosting D&D game nights.
